Apple Pie by Grandma Ople

All credits for the following recipe go to the original creator who shared the recipe at the following link: Apple Pie by Grandma Ople

Ingredients:

  • 8 small Granny Smith apples, or as needed
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 (9 inch) double-crust pie pastry, thawed
